Our Story

Craft Built Over Twenty Years

Lee Marshall started in the trade straight from school. He learned the old way, by doing it. Two decades later, he still works on every project himself.

When you hire Marshall & Co., you get Lee. Not a site manager you have never met, not a subcontractor drafted in at short notice. The same pair of hands from the first visit to the final finish.
