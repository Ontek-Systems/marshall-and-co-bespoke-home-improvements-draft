Statement Joinery

Built Into the Room, Not Just Added

The difference between a media wall that looks designed and one that looks assembled is how it's built into the room. Lee Marshall builds media walls that account for the architecture around them, including ceiling height, alcoves, chimney breasts, and the proportions of the space, which all factor into how the unit is designed.

Integrated lighting, concealed cable management, floating shelves, cabinet storage, and fire surrounds are all available within the design. The brief is set early and the joinery is crafted to it exactly, meaning no last-minute adjustments on site because something wasn't measured properly.

Interior panelling, whether Shaker-style dado panels, full-height fluted panels, or custom batten designs, follows the same principle. Every profile, spacing, and depth is agreed in advance and executed to a standard you can feel with your hand.
