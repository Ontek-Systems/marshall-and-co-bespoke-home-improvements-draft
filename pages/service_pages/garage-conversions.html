Convert Your Garage

A single or double garage in a Perthshire property is often the easiest way to add a genuine room without touching the existing footprint or navigating complex planning applications. In most cases it qualifies as Permitted Development.

Lee Marshall has converted garages into home offices, playrooms, gyms, utility rooms, and annexes. The structural work varies depending on the garage type, whether integral, attached, or detached, but Lee assesses this at the outset and quotes accurately for the specific structure you have.

Insulation, internal lining, electrics, heating, flooring, and joinery are all handled in-house. When the project completes, the room is finished and ready to use, rather than a shell waiting for the next trade to arrive.
