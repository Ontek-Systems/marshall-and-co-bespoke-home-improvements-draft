Your Garden, Fully Utilised

Scotland's climate demands a garden room that is genuinely weatherproof and well insulated, not a glorified shed. Marshall & Co. builds garden rooms that are warm in January, comfortable in July, and solid enough to stay that way for twenty years.

Lee handles the full build from groundworks up: base preparation, framing, cladding, roofing, insulation, internal lining, electrics, and flooring. Popular uses across Perthshire include home offices with a proper power supply and broadband, art studios, home gyms, and leisure retreats. Whatever the brief, Lee builds it to the same standard.
