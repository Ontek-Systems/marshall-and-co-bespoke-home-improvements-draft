Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Marshall & Co. Can't find your answer? Give Lee a call and he'll be happy to help.

Get in Touch
What types of projects do you undertake?
Marshall & Co. undertakes a wide range of home improvement projects including structural extensions, loft conversions, full property renovations, bespoke joinery and interior fit-outs, and garden rooms. Whether it's a single-room transformation or a complete whole-house renovation, Lee has the expertise to deliver.
Do you work on both traditional and modern properties?
Yes. Over 20 years working across Perth and Perthshire means Lee has extensive experience with traditional sandstone properties, Victorian terraces, and modern new builds alike. Each property type has its own character and requirements, and Marshall & Co. has the knowledge to approach them correctly.
How is project pricing determined?
All pricing is tailored to the specific scope of each project. Following an initial consultation and site visit, Lee provides a fully itemised, transparent quotation that covers all agreed works. There are no vague estimates and no hidden costs. Every charge is explained upfront before any work begins.
Can you assist with planning permission applications?
Marshall & Co. regularly works alongside architects and planning consultants and can guide you through the process or introduce you to trusted professionals. Lee understands local planning requirements in Perth and Perthshire and can advise on what approvals are likely needed before you commit to your project.
Are you fully insured?
Yes. Marshall & Co. carries full public liability insurance and all works are carried out in compliance with current building regulations and health and safety requirements. You can have complete confidence in the professional standards applied to every project.
How long does a typical project take?
Project duration varies depending on scope and complexity. A single-room renovation or joinery installation might take a few days to a couple of weeks. A full extension or whole-house renovation could take several months. A realistic, agreed programme is provided at the outset and progress is communicated clearly throughout.
Will Lee Marshall be on site personally?
Yes. Lee is personally involved in every project Marshall & Co. takes on. He is the person who quoted the job, the person doing or directly overseeing the work, and the person responsible for the finished result. You won't be passed to a stranger or left chasing someone you've never met.
Which areas do you cover?
Marshall & Co. is based in Perth and regularly works throughout Perthshire and the surrounding region, including Dundee, Auchterarder, Kinross, Crieff, Blairgowrie, Dunblane, and Coupar Angus. If you are unsure whether your area is covered, call Lee directly and he will let you know.
How quickly can work begin?
Start dates depend on the current programme and the scale of your project. Lee typically aims to provide a proposed start date within a few working days of completing the site visit and agreeing scope. For urgent projects, call directly and he will do his best to accommodate your timescale.
How do I get a quote?
Simply call Lee on 07940 568877 or fill in the contact form on our contact page. He will get back to you promptly to arrange a free, no-obligation consultation. From there, a site visit and fully detailed written quotation follow. There is no commitment required at any stage.
